Cake Sales

Our Cake sales app provides customers with a seamless shopping experience, with a user-friendly interface dedicated to browsing and buying cake online. With an integrated online payment system, shoppers can complete transactions securely and easily, ensuring convenience and peace of mind. Furthermore, our platform includes an intuitive admin panel, giving administrators complete control to manage products with ease. From adding new items to editing existing listings or removing outdated items, the admin panel simplifies inventory management for seamless operations. With a focus on customer satisfaction and efficient management, our app caters to the needs of both buyers and sellers in the ever-evolving e-commerce landscape.

Technogy used: Flutter Firebase C# ASP.NET Core API
